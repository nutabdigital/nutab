Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our graphic & brand design services.

What is brand design?

Brand design is the process of creating a visual identity for your business, including logos, colors, fonts, and style guidelines.

Do you offer logo design?

Yes, we create custom logos that reflect your brand's personality and values.

Can you design graphics for social media?

Absolutely — we design assets optimized for web and social platforms.

How does professional brand design help my business?

Professional brand design builds trust, increases recognition, and helps your business stand out in competitive markets.

What is included in a brand identity package?

Our brand identity packages include logo design, color palettes, typography, brand guidelines, and digital/print assets.

Can you refresh or modernize my existing brand?

Yes, we offer brand refresh services to update and align your visual identity with current trends and business goals.

Will I own the rights to my brand assets?

You receive full ownership and usage rights for all brand assets we create for your business.

How long does the brand design process take?

Most brand design projects are completed in 2-4 weeks, depending on scope and feedback cycles.

Do you offer brand design services in Calgary?

Yes! NuTab Digital is a Calgary-based creative agency specializing in logo design, brand identity, and graphic design for Alberta businesses.

Can I meet with your Calgary design team in person?

Absolutely. Our Calgary team is available for in-person consultations, creative reviews, and collaborative workshops with local clients.

Do you work with Calgary small businesses and startups?

Yes, we create brand identities for Calgary businesses of all sizes, from startups to established companies looking to refresh their visual presence.
