Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our e‑commerce development services.

What is e-commerce development?

E-commerce development involves creating online stores and platforms that allow businesses to sell products or services online, including catalogs, carts, and payment gateways.

How long does it take to build an e-commerce site?

Timelines depend on complexity; simple stores can be delivered in weeks, complex platforms in months.

Can you integrate payment gateways?

Yes — we integrate gateways like Stripe, PayPal, and enterprise payment providers.

How much does it cost to build an e-commerce website?

Costs vary based on features, platform, and design. We offer solutions for every budget and provide transparent quotes.

Will my online store be optimized for SEO?

Yes, we build e-commerce sites with SEO best practices to help your products rank higher and attract more customers.

Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify or WooCommerce?

Absolutely. We handle migrations to Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms with minimal downtime and data loss.

Is my e-commerce site secure for customer payments?

We implement PCI-compliant payment integrations and security protocols to protect customer data and transactions.

Do you provide ongoing support and updates?

Yes, we offer maintenance, analytics, and feature improvements to keep your store competitive and running smoothly.

Do you build e-commerce websites for Calgary businesses?

Yes! NuTab Digital is Calgary-based and specializes in e-commerce development for local Alberta businesses. We build Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom online stores.

Can you help Calgary retailers sell online?

Absolutely. We help Calgary retail businesses expand online with professional e-commerce websites, local SEO, and secure payment integrations.

Do you offer local Calgary e-commerce support?

Yes, our Calgary team provides ongoing support, training, and optimization for local e-commerce businesses. We're available for in-person meetings and consultations.
